The Ethics of Belief

[PRESENTER NOTES]
This marks our entry into Unit 2 of this course. In one way, today’s lecture will be very different from what’s come before while in another way, it really won’t. We’re no longer going to be concerned with whether God exists – i.e. we’re not doing metaphysics. We’re going to talk about what we should believe about the existence of God. So the topic will stay the same, while the focus will shift. Later in this unit, we’ll turn away from questions about God and look at a variety of other topics in Epistemology – questions about belief and evidence and justification and knowledge.
