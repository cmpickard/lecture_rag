How might we respond to this argument? Well, P2 is very nearly true by definition, so the obvious target is P1. So we should ask ourselves… is it rational to trust people merely because you agree with them on political matters? That’s really what’s at issue here. That’s the place to press back against Rini, if you think she’s wrong about this.
